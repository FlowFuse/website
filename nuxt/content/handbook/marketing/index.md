# Marketing department

## What we do

We're the team that promotes our company and product of FlowFuse to the 
[core audience](/handbook/marketing/messaging/#ideal-customer-profile-icp)
of FlowFuse.

There's 3 pillars we're heavily invested in:
1. Our [Content Strategy](./content-strategy/)
2. Account Based Marketing
3. [Events](./events.md)

Combined, these pillars are engaging with our audience at different stages of
their buying journey, and also post-sale.

The monthly budget of the department including headcount and program budget should be equal to 50-60% of monthly pipeline generated.

### Marketing Metrics


The ultimate goal, and thus KPI, for the department is to
[generate sales pipeline](https://app-eu1.hubspot.com/reports-list/26586079/198880430/).

Supporting metrics we track are:
- [FFC signups](https://product-metrics.flowfuse.cloud/dashboard/product)
- [Contact Sales form completions](https://app-eu1.hubspot.com/submissions/26586079/form/734455e5-4cda-4329-97da-07e40cda791c/performance/)
- [Unique website visitors](https://eu.posthog.com/project/2209/insights/PXeb0YSF)

Each of these metrics is reported on weekly during our Marketing Team Meeting
to ensure we're tracking progress toward our KPI.
