OPC UA has become the gold standard for secure, reliable industrial communication, but deploying and managing it at scale across distributed devices remains a significant challenge for many organizations.

<!--more-->

Without the right tooling, teams are left wrestling with complex configurations, inconsistent security policies, and fragmented device management that slows down digital transformation.

By combining the flexibility of Node-RED with enterprise-grade device management, FlowFuse makes it practical to deploy OPC UA connections securely across your entire industrial estate, from a single machine to thousands of endpoints.

In this webinar, Kristopher Sandoval and Etienne Rossignon, creator of NodeOPCUA, walked through how to connect industrial devices using OPC UA in a secure, scalable, and maintainable way, reducing complexity without sacrificing the robustness that industrial environments demand.

Etienne and Kristopher covered:

- What NodeOPCUA is and why OPC UA has become the preferred protocol for secure industrial connectivity
- The common challenges of deploying OPC UA at scale and how FlowFuse + NodeOPCUA addresses them
- How to configure client/server connections securely within FlowFuse
- Managing security certificates and authentication across a fleet of devices, including managed certificate renewal and GDS enrollment
- How the Explore node replaces dozens of manual reads with a single operation, returning the full OPC UA hierarchy as a structured JSON object
- Namespace aliasing for stable, reusable node references across deployments
- Configuration-driven node ID subscriptions, passing arrays of node IDs programmatically instead of selecting through the UI
- A layered security approach: OPC UA certificate management + FlowFuse encryption at rest and in transit + role-based access control

This session is ideal for OT/IT engineers, automation architects, and industrial IoT practitioners looking to modernize their device connectivity strategy and get more from their OPC UA infrastructure.
