OPC UA has become the gold standard for secure, reliable industrial communication — but deploying and managing it at scale across distributed devices remains a significant challenge for many organizations.

<!--more-->

Without the right tooling, teams are left wrestling with complex configurations, inconsistent security policies, and fragmented device management that slows down digital transformation.

By combining the flexibility of Node-RED with enterprise-grade device management, FlowFuse makes it practical to deploy OPC UA connections securely across your entire industrial estate — from a single machine to thousands of endpoints.

In this webinar we will explore how FlowFuse enables teams to connect industrial devices using OPC UA in a secure, scalable, and maintainable way — reducing complexity without sacrificing the robustness that industrial environments demand.

In this session, Kristopher will discuss:

- What OPC UA is and why it has become the preferred protocol for secure industrial connectivity
- The common challenges of deploying OPC UA at scale and how FlowFuse addresses them
- How to configure OPC UA client/server connections within Node-RED on FlowFuse
- Managing security certificates and authentication across a fleet of devices
- Best practices for scaling OPC UA deployments in real-world industrial environments
- [Live Demo] — A walkthrough of connecting and managing OPC UA-enabled devices using FlowFuse in a real deployment scenario

This webinar is ideal for OT/IT engineers, automation architects, and industrial IoT practitioners who are looking to modernise their device connectivity strategy and get more from their OPC UA infrastructure. Don't miss out on joining us live to see firsthand how FlowFuse can simplify secure industrial connectivity at scale — and ask your questions directly to our expert during the live Q&A.
